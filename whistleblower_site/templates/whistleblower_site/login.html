Welcome to OrgReport

OrgReport enables students, particularly those at UVA, to report harmful or inappropriate behavior within various student organizations on college campuses. It offers optional anonymity, aiming to create a safe environment for disclosure and promote a culture of safety and accountability.

This site is not an actual UVA/Public resource. It is a project for CS3240.
